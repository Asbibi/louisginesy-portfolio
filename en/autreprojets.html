Illustrative Posters for School Clubs

During the 3 years I've spent at Telecom Sudparis, I joined several clubs and associations in which I often had a position related to communication. This way, I had the occasion to produce several illustrative posters for those clubs mainly using the Gimp software.

  • BDA - Festiv'Art

    Festiv'Art is an event organized by Telecom Sudparis's Art Office (BDA). It consists into several activities and workshops spread over a week. This is the poster I created to introduce the activities of the first day.

  • BDA - Presentation

    To ensure its renewal, it's fundamental for the Art Office (BDA) to interest and integrate new students as early as possible in the shcoolar year. This is why a presentation of the different positions in the Art Office is organized, and this is the poster I made to announce the presentation.

  • Gamint - Lists' Battle

    Every year, Gamint organizes a LAN on the game "League of Legends", which is our most popular LAN. On this occasion, we set up a tournament on this game reserved to the students canditating to the Student Office (BDE), and cast it to the other students.

  • Gamint - The Enderlan

    Poster announcing the first edition of a LAN on Minecraft, for which we had prepared a dedicated private server. I realized the background of this poster on Blender, as well as the model for the 3D printed trophee offered to the LAN winners. Both showcase an enderman (an iconic monster from Minecraft) holding a diamond pickaxe.

  • Gamint - Party Games LAN

    My first poster as communication manager at Gamint. This LAN targetted a set of several multiplayer games qucik to learn and aimed to announce and celebrate the new team incharge of the club.

  • Gamint - Smash LAN

    The poster for our first LAN as second year students, I created it with a first year student.

  • Gamint - My first LAN Poster

    My first associative poster ever, its design is inspired from a poster from the previous Gamint team.

  • GATE Project - Brainstorming Fresqu'It

    I've done this poster to announce the brainstorming session of our GATE project Fresqu'It. Indeed, as our project is about painting a wall with the help of teenagers from Evry, this brainstorming aimed at choosing the fresco theme with them.

  • Le RIG - Formation 1 et 2 sur 6

    When I created the 3D club of my school, Le RIG, I provided a training of the software Blender in order to teach the basics of 3D modelling and animation to the interested students. This poster advertised the 2 first sessions that were about the modelisation part.

  • Le RIG - Formation 5 et 6 sur 6

    And this is the poster for the two last sessions. They were about shaders, lighting and compositing/post-process. I also made an animated poster for the third and fourth sessions that were about rigging and animating.

GATE Project - Fresqu'It'

The GATE project is a group project mandatory for first year students of Telecom Sudparis. The subjects are various and not necessarly related to a technical topic.
I decided to join the Fresqu'It project. We were 7 students aiming to produce a mural painting in a tunnel at Evry. An peculiarity of that project being that we didn't want to paint this fresco alone, but with the participation of children and teenagers living in Evry.
During this project, another student and me were in charge of the external communication, and here is the sum up video that we've realized and the end of the project.
